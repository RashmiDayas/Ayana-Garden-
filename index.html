The Concept

The Art of Doing Nothing

Ella Town is for the crowd. Kithalella is for the soul. Located at an elevation of 1,041 meters, Ayana Garden is a transition zone where the mist meets the mountains. We are not just a place to sleep; we are a basecamp for "Slow Travel." Whether you are hiking Stage 16 of the Pekoe Trail, waiting for the iconic blue train, or simply sipping coffee while watching the mist roll over Ella Rock.

Read

Curated reading nooks & mini-library for your quiet moments.

Watch

Home to 34 endemic bird species visiting our garden.

Breathe

Pure mountain air at 1,041 meters, away from the traffic.

Experiences

More Than Just a View

Here is how you can slow down with us.

On-Site USP

A Birdwatcher’s Secret

Kithalella is an ornithological bridge between the wet zone and the highlands. You don't need to go far to see the rarest birds; they come to our garden.

The Ayana List: Home to the Sri Lanka Hanging Parrot, Grey Hornbill, and Yellow-fronted Barbet.

Complimentary Binoculars & Bird Checklist available.

Hiking

The Pekoe Trail - Stage 16

We are located directly on the route. Hiking from Demodara to Ella? We offer a Free Hiker’s Pickup from the trail end to our bungalow.

Perfect for hikers.

Walking

The Railway Hike

Just 500m to the quiet Kithalella Railway Station. Walk along the tracks to the famous Nine Arches Bridge (40-minute scenic walk) or sit at the station and watch the blue train pass by without the crowds.

Scenic & Crowd-free.

Exploration

Secret Waterfalls

Avoid the tourist traps. Ask our host for the hand-drawn map to the hidden waterfalls and bathing spots that are not found on Google Maps.

Exclusive local knowledge.
